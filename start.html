Where to Start

Listed below are the most common types of questions that you will see on the NYS Regents Algebra 1 examination.

  1. Factoring algebriac expressions using greatest common factor

  2. Factoring algebraic expressions using the product and sum method with a leading coefficient of 1

  3. Factoring quadratic expressions using the product and sum method with a leading coefficient greater than one

  4. Using the relationship between the factors of an algebraic expression to determine the zeros of a function
